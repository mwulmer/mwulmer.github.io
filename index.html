Maximilian Ulmer

Hello! I am a PhD candidate at the Munich School of Robotics and Machine Intelligence of the Technical University of Munich. My research is focused on perception and control for robotic manipulation.

Prior, I did my M.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering with a focus on automation and robotics at TUM, where I worked with Prof. Diepold.

Research

My research is focused on moving robotic manipulation from the lab to practice. Much of my work is about learning in the physical world, computer vision, control, reinforcement learning & optimal control.
